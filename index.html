Top three activities to do in San Antonio

Visit the Alamo

Take a tour of this historic Texas landmark and soak in the history!

Walk down the riverwalk

The riverwalk is a vibrant area full of shops and music. You can even ride a boat down the river!

Attend a Spurs game

The Spurs are the main sports team of San Antonio. Cheer on the Spurs at the Frost Bank Center!
